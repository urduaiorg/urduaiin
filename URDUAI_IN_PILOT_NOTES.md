# urduai.in Pilot Notes

## Independent research takeaway

The India opportunity is strong enough for a lean pilot, not yet for an aggressive scale-up.

What appears true:

- India has a large Urdu-speaking audience, even using conservative Census framing.
- AI adoption in India is already mainstream enough to justify focused educational content.
- There is no obvious dominant India-first Urdu AI editorial brand.
- Urdu-script search demand is weak compared with Roman Urdu, Hindi, and English mixed behavior.
- AdSense is not the primary business case; authority, audience, and partnerships matter more.

## Product direction

`urduai.in` should be built as:

- India-first
- Urdu-first in reading experience
- mixed-language in SEO logic
- educational rather than generic news-first
- strongest for students, freelancers, teachers, and mobile users

## Pilot goals

This pilot is intended to validate:

- whether Indian Urdu readers respond to the positioning
- whether mixed Urdu/Hindi/English SEO can gain traction
- whether the site structure is strong enough for high-value publishing
- whether the domain can become a serious India-focused content property

## First pages to prioritize

- Home
- Start Here
- AI Basics
- What is ChatGPT?
- Free AI Tools
- AI for Students
- Gemini
- AI News / Newsletter

## Success criteria

- strong local review from team
- clear India-first differentiation from urduai.org
- high-quality Urdu reading experience
- deployable to Cloudflare Pages with minimal friction
